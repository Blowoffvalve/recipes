Ingredients:
	- Ramen noodles
	- Some protein.
	
Directions:
	- Boil water
	- Prepare protein to only require mild reheating to be ready
	- Place noodles inside the water and boil for 2 minutes.
	- Drain excess water from the pot.
	- Add protein to pot
	- Add spices within noodle satchets to the pot.
	- Cook noodles till they're soft.
	- Brunch/Dinner is served
